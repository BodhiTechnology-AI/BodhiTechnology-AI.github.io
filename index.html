Frequently Asked Questions

What makes your solutions customizable?
We tailor every solution — hardware circuits, firmware code, and software applications — to your exact requirements using Rust for embedded reliability. No off-the-shelf products; everything is purpose-built for you.
Why do you use Rust for hardware?
Rust provides memory safety without garbage collection, zero-cost abstractions, and fearless concurrency — making it ideal for embedded systems where crashes or memory bugs can have real-world consequences. Our Rust-powered devices are more reliable and secure.
How long does a typical project take?
Timelines vary by complexity. Simple Arduino/IoT prototypes can be ready in 2–4 weeks. Complex custom embedded systems typically take 6–12 weeks from requirements to delivery. We provide a timeline estimate after your initial consultation.
Do you provide support after delivery?
Yes! We offer post-deployment support, bug fixes, firmware updates, and maintenance contracts. Client satisfaction doesn't end at delivery — it's an ongoing commitment.
Who can apply for the internship?
Diploma and engineering students from Electronics, CS, IT, and Instrumentation branches are welcome. Passion for technology and eagerness to learn are the only prerequisites — no prior professional experience needed.
